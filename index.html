WHY

“To survive and prosper, we need healthy oceans. The ocean is the largest ecosystem on Earth, it is the planet’s life support system. Oceans generate half of the oxygen we breathe and, at any given moment, they contain more than 97% of the world’s water. Oceans provide at least a sixth of the animal protein people eat. Living oceans absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and reduce climate change impacts. The diversity and productivity of the world’s oceans is a vital interest for humankind. Our security, our economy, our very survival all require healthy oceans.”

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to keep our oceans as clean as possible by creating solutions to current pollution issues and spreading awareness of oceanic issues. We hope to create healthy, rich and biodiverse oceans for future generations to enjoy. Our company focuses on saving the ocean and to make sure that the environment is back to how it was years ago but we can’t do it if not everyone is involved and helping out. There are many things that we can do and there are many things that makes us all different but one of the many things that unites us is our ocean, our world and our environment. Let’s work together to make sure that we protect what is ours.

Concerns

People around the world are unaware of the problems of certain items we use everyday. Plastic is everywhere: Soda Cans, Water Bottles, Straws, and Bags. For years now we have been told and aware that recycling is the best way of getting rid of waste in the best way possible and still 90% of global plastic is not being recycled. Oceans like the Antarctic ocean supports many sea creatures such as- Penguins, Whales, Fishes, and Seals.

Solutions

Reduce Carbon Footprint! Carbon dioxide, a known greenhouse gas, is making our oceans more acidic. This is contributing to the loss of corals on a global scale, as their calcium skeletons are weakened by the increasing acidity of the water. To fix this, leave no trash behind. As beach crowds increase, so does the amount of trash left behind or blown away. Be sure to properly reduce, reuse, and recycle anything you wish to dispose of.
